Running Today’s Business While Building Tomorrow

Summarizes practical ideas for balancing day-to-day responsibilities and long-term planning, and overall growth.


Who is Sabrina Klor?

Sabrina Klor is the current CEO of 10 Design. She if recognized for her effective approach to leadership and business-building. The article paraphrases her methods for staying effective daily while still investing time into the future.


Main Message from Sabrina Klor

Successful growth comes from balancing immediate tasks with long- term strategy. Rather than focusing on the present, the article emphasizes the use of planning systems and priorities that allow a business to develop.


Key Takeaways from the article

  1. Reserve time for long-term planning, even while busy.
  2. Create repeatable systems with clear instructions, so daily operations won’t consume attention.
  3. Set measurable goals that are related to today’s work and the future’s progress.
